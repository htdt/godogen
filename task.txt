Top-down racing game with procedural terrain.
Primary technical focus: Road must visually conform to terrain surface using decal projection (not floating geometry). The track is painted onto the heightmap terrain, following its contours naturally over hills and through valleys.
Track: Closed-loop circuit with natural winding layout—hairpins, sweeping turns, S-curves. Road surface affects gameplay: fast on track, slow on grass/dirt.
Terrain: Heightmap with distinct elevation changes. Hills create ramps where the car can catch air (visible jumping/landing physics). Mountain walls as track borders.
Environment: Instanced tree forests lining the track, properly scaled.
